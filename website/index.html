Risk Detection

The clauses that cost people most.

Cautelio flags the language that lawyers notice and everyone else misses.

High Risk

IP Assignment

Clauses that transfer ownership of your work, ideas, or inventions to the other party — including work done outside the contract.

High Risk

Non-Compete

Restrictions on who you can work with, for how long, and in what field — often broader than they appear.

High Risk

Auto-Renewal Traps

Contracts that renew automatically with a short cancellation window you're likely to miss.

Medium Risk

Broad Indemnification

Making you liable for the other party's legal costs and damages, even when you're not at fault.

Medium Risk

Mandatory Arbitration

Waiving your right to sue in court or join a class action — forcing disputes into private arbitration.

Low Risk

One-Sided Liability Caps

Limits on what they owe you for damages, with no equivalent limit on what you owe them.
