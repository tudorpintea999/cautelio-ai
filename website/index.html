Risk Detection

The clauses that cost people most.

Cautelio flags the language that lawyers notice and everyone else misses.

High Risk

IP Assignment

Clauses that transfer ownership of your work, ideas, or inventions — including work done outside the contract scope.

High Risk

Non-Compete

Restrictions on who you can work with, for how long, and in what field — often far broader than they appear.

High Risk

Auto-Renewal Traps

Contracts that renew automatically with a cancellation window buried in the fine print you'll miss.

Medium Risk

Broad Indemnification

Making you liable for the other party's legal costs and damages, even when you're not at fault.

Medium Risk

Mandatory Arbitration

Waiving your right to sue in court — forcing all disputes into private arbitration that favors the other side.

Low Risk

One-Sided Liability Caps

Limits on what they owe you for damages, with no equivalent limit on what you owe them.

FAQ

Common questions.

Does Cautelio store my contracts?

No. Contract text is sent for analysis and discarded immediately. Nothing is stored on our servers. See the privacy policy.

What types of contracts does it work on?

Any contract visible in Chrome — freelance agreements, vendor contracts, SaaS terms, NDAs, employment contracts. Works on DocuSign, Google Docs, PDFs, and plain web pages.

Is this a replacement for a lawyer?

No, and we won't pretend it is. Cautelio helps you understand what you're signing and flag what needs attention. For high-stakes contracts, always consult a lawyer. But for the dozens of routine agreements you sign every year, Cautelio is the layer of protection you've been missing.

How accurate is the analysis?

Cautelio uses Claude, one of the most capable AI models available. It is accurate on standard contract language. It can miss nuance in highly unusual or jurisdiction-specific clauses — which is why we always recommend human review for anything high-stakes.
